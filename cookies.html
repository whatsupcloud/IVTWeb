How cookie and similar technologies may be used on this website

Effective date: March 11, 2023. This Cookie Policy explains how Involytics Technology may use cookies, local storage, server logs, tags, pixels, and comparable technologies to support website functionality, security, analytics readiness, and user experience.

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files or similar identifiers stored on your browser, device, or application environment when you visit a website. Related technologies such as local storage, session tokens, pixels, embedded scripts, and server-side identifiers may perform similar functions and are treated similarly for the purpose of this policy where applicable.

2. Why We Use Cookie-Related Technologies

Cookie and similar technologies may be used to maintain basic website functionality, improve performance, remember technical preferences, detect misuse, protect systems against abuse, enable diagnostics, understand traffic patterns, and support future analytics or service improvements. In some situations, certain technologies may be necessary to ensure security, reliability, or proper rendering of the website.

3. Categories of Cookies or Similar Technologies

We may use or later introduce strictly necessary cookies, performance-related cookies, functionality cookies, analytics cookies, security or fraud-detection identifiers, and similar technical storage methods. Strictly necessary technologies help core website operation. Performance or analytics technologies help us understand traffic or behaviour trends. Functionality technologies may remember preferences or interface settings. Security technologies may assist with log correlation, threat detection, rate control, and system integrity.

4. Current Website Use

As of the effective date of this policy, this website primarily relies on essential technical behaviour, browser capabilities, and server-side or client-side functionality needed for page rendering, navigation, and security-related operation. If analytics, advertising, remarketing, advanced tracking, consent managers, or embedded third-party scripts are introduced later, this policy may be updated to reflect those implementations.

5. Consent and Browser Controls

Where required by applicable law or best practice, your consent may be requested before non-essential cookies or similar technologies are activated. You may also configure your browser or device settings to block, limit, or delete cookies. However, disabling certain technologies may affect website functionality, security, performance, remembered preferences, or compatibility with some parts of the website.

6. Third-Party Technologies

Some third-party tools, hosting platforms, embedded media providers, analytics services, map providers, communication widgets, or security services may independently use cookies or similar identifiers when their content or infrastructure is loaded through our website. We do not control all such third-party technologies and recommend that you review their policies where relevant.

7. Log Files and Device Information

In addition to browser-stored technologies, our systems or service providers may automatically record technical and security-related information such as IP addresses, timestamps, user-agent strings, approximate geolocation indicators, request paths, referrers, and device or browser attributes. This information may be used for reliability monitoring, capacity planning, security review, misuse detection, and troubleshooting.

8. Data Retention and Security

Information associated with cookie or similar technology use may be retained for periods reasonably necessary for system operation, analytics review, technical troubleshooting, security assessment, or regulatory compliance. We use reasonable safeguards to protect such information, although no online system can guarantee absolute security.

9. Your Choices

You may clear cookies through browser settings, use private-browsing modes, limit permissions for storage or scripts, disable third-party cookies where available, and revisit browser or device controls from time to time. If we implement a consent banner or preferences tool in the future, such controls may provide an additional mechanism for managing your preferences.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in website functionality, third-party integrations, legal requirements, or operational practices. The latest version posted on this website, together with its effective date, will apply from the time of publication unless otherwise stated.
